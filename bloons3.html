Bloons TD Battles 2 (BTDB2) is a head-to-head competitive tower defense game based on the engine of Bloons TD 6. Your goal is to pop the Bloons on your track while sending Bloons to overwhelm your opponent's defense and deplete their lives!

CORE CONCEPTS

Success in this Tower defense game relies on three core mechanics: **Defense**, **Offense (Rushing)**, and **Economy (Farming)**.

  • Defense: Place and upgrade **Monkey Towers** along the path to pop incoming Bloons. Each tower has three upgrade paths.
  • Offense (Rushing): Spend money to send grouped or high-tier Bloons to your opponent's track. This is the main method to force them to spend resources defensively.
  • Economy: Generate extra passive income (Eco) by sending **Eco Bloons** or by using **Banana Farms** to gain an advantage in resource accumulation.

BEGINNER STRATEGIES üéØ

Focus on **consistent defense** and **stable economy** while learning which towers counter specific Bloon types.

  • Start with Dart/Boomerang: Two un-upgraded **Dart Monkeys** or a **Boomerang Monkey** can typically handle the first few rounds easily.
  • Place Your Hero Early: Heroes like **Sauda** or **Quincy** should be placed immediately to maximize their level gain for mid-game rushes.
  • Economic Consistency: If you are not using **Banana Farms**, send **Grouped Pink Bloons** consistently (available from Round 5-6) to build your passive income (Eco) quickly.
  • Know Your Counters:
    **Lead Bloons** (Round 13) require explosives or high-tier damage. **Camo Bloons** (Round 11) require specific detection from towers like the **Ninja Monkey** or a **Monkey Village**.

ADVANCED STRATEGIES üí∞

These tactics focus on precise micro, timing, and exploiting the massive income potential of the late-game.

  • Farm Sustainably (Banks/BRF): Master the use of the **Monkey Bank** (x-3-x **Banana Farm**) for interest, or the **Banana Research Facility** (4-x-x) for raw production.
  • The Early All-In: Utilizing early-game rushes like **Grouped Yellows** or **Grouped Black Bloons** (Round 11) to catch a greedy opponent off-guard who spent too much on farming.
  • Bloon Layering: Send a mix of different Bloon types that exploit your opponent's defensive weaknesses simultaneously. For example, overwhelming a single-target defense with **Regen Rainbows** and **Ceramics**.
  • DDT and ZOMG Rushes: The most common late-game win condition is the **MOAB-Class Bloon** rush. The **DDT** (Round 26) is the most challenging, requiring towers with high damage, Camo detection, and Lead popping power.
  • Ability Micro: Precisely timing powerful, active abilities (like Overclock or First Strike) on the strongest defensive towers to save against massive, resource-draining rushes.
