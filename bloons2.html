Bloons TD Battles 6 (BTD6) is a tower defense game where two players compete head-to-head. Unlike the original BTD6, your goal is to pop the Bloons on your track while also sending Bloons to overwhelm your opponent's defense and deplete their lives!

CORE CONCEPTS

The game revolves around three core mechanics: **Defense**, **Offense (Rushing)**, and **Economy (Farming)**.

  • Defense: Place and upgrade **Monkey Towers** along the path to pop incoming Bloons. Each tower has three upgrade paths, allowing you to choose powerful specialized roles.
  • Offense (Rushing): Spend money to send Bloons to your opponent's track. Overwhelming them with high-tier or grouped Bloons is the primary way to win.
  • Economy: Generate extra income (money) throughout the game. You gain income from popping Bloons and, more importantly, from **Banana Farms** or **Eco** (sending eco-Bloons like Pink Bloons for passive income).

BEGINNER STRATEGIES üéØ

Focus on **consistent defense** and **stable economy** while learning which towers counter which Bloon types (Lead, Camo, Ceramic, MOAB-class).

  • Start Strong with Dart Monkeys: Two un-upgraded Dart Monkeys ($215 each) can handle the first few rounds easily, buying time to build your economy.
  • Place Your Hero Early: Heroes like **Quincy** (great all-rounder) or **Sauda** (excellent early-game crowd control) level up automatically each round. Place them early to maximize their power for mid-game rushes.
  • Focus on Tier 3 Upgrades: Don't invest all your cash into a single powerful tower. A few affordable Tier 3 upgrades (like a 4-0-2 **Ninja Monkey** or a 2-0-4 **Sniper Monkey**) often provide better value and map coverage.
  • Know Your Counters:
    **Lead Bloons** (Round 28) require Bomb/Mortar/T3+ Dart Monkeys. **Camo Bloons** (Round 24) require Ninja/Sniper/Camo detection from a Monkey Village.
  • Aggressive Eco-Pinks: If you are not using Banana Farms, send **Grouped Pink Bloons** (available from Round 5-6) consistently to build your passive income (Eco) quickly.

ADVANCED STRATEGIES üí∞

These strategies require more precise micro-management, deep knowledge of Bloon rush timings, and the ability to balance offense and defense.

  • Farm Sustainably (Bank/BRF): Master the use of the **Monkey Bank** (x-3-x Banana Farm) or **Banana Research Facility** (4-x-x Banana Farm). The Bank allows you to collect interest, multiplying your money.
  • The Anti-Stall: Actively try to prevent the game from dragging on when you are losing the farming race. This often involves sending an early, unexpected rush (like **Grouped MOABs** on Round 18) to force an immediate response.
  • Bloon Layering: Send a mix of different Bloon types that exploit your opponent's defensive weaknesses at the same time. For example, sending **Regen Leads** followed immediately by **Regen Zebras** (Round 13) can overwhelm an area-of-effect defense.
  • Paragon Preparation: For late-game "Freeplay" or Boss Bloon challenges, planning for and accumulating the resources needed for a **Paragon** (Tier 6 tower) is essential. This includes placing enough of the base tower and gaining enough pops.
  • Micro Abilities: Efficiently using powerful abilities like the **Spike Storm** (x-4-x Spike Factory) or the **Overclock** (x-4-x Engineer Monkey) on the strongest tower for maximum uptime, which can save a massive rush.
